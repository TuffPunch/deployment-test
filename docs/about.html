  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginning

    The club was founded in March 2014 by a group of ambitious and motivated students of the Higher Institute of Technological Studies of Charguia under the name "Club MobileDev ISET Charguia".
    As its name indicates, the activity of the Club was focused mainly on mobile development offering basic and detailed training for students and ensuring the organization of several events and conferences on the subject. The club won several titles and competitions like the "WebDays" competition for the years 2014 and 2015.

  • 2015-2018

    The Radical Change

    In 2015, the club became a member of a larger community known as the "Google Developers Group" (GDG) allowing it to expand its activities and enter into collaboration with other clubs. Thus, the name of the club became "ISET'CH Google Club".

  • 2019-Now

    The Legacy Continue...

    After one year of being inactive in 2019 the club was back with new bloods and started everything from scratch yet they made couple events before the lockdown came and it was the best opportunity to shine where the club made several online sessions and workshops.
    Then they started to shape the new era of the club by making educational content and dedicating a whole department that takes care of the IT skills of the members
